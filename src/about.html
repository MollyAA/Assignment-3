How We Started

It all started in 1970. Our founder Ben Buzzy always had in an intrest in bees and sweets. He knew that raw honey was the absolute way to go so, he saved up all he could to open his own bee farm. He sold his honey in old sauce jars and paper labels. One day he met Juniper Daisy and she knew this is where she was supposed to be. She joined the team and transformed the packaging with beatiful hand crafted masion jars, real sticker labels and ribbon. After that it was all history. Our mission is to make people smile and bee healthy!
